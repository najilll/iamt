Welcome to IAMT-Institute of Advanced Mobile and Tech

At IAMT, we are dedicated to shaping the future of mobile technology professionals. Located in Malappuram, Kerala, our institute offers top-tier training in smartphone servicing, backed by over 17 years of industry and technical expertise. Our comprehensive programs, led by certified trainers, cover advanced chip-level repairs and the latest in smartphone technology. We pride ourselves on providing a 100% job placement assurance, ensuring our graduates are equipped for success. With state-of-the-art facilities, practical on-the-job training, and additional courses in computer hardware and languages, IAMT is your gateway to a thriving career in mobile technology. Join us to transform your passion into a thriving career.
